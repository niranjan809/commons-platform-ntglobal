    ❓ Help & FAQ
    🚶 Moving around

    Use the arrow keys or WASD to walk, or just click anywhere on the meadow to walk there. The whole meadow fits your screen, so your avatar is always visible.

    🟢 Status & breaks

    Set your status (Available / Busy / Break / Away) from the sidebar. For a break, pick a type (lunch, coffee, BRB, focus, call), a “back in” time, and an optional note — teammates see your countdown and note on your avatar and in the list. You’re switched back to Available automatically when the timer ends.

    ⚡ Instant meeting (Zoho)

    Click ⚡ Instant meeting — a Zoho meeting is created, opens for you, and the join link is dropped into the current chat channel (and Slack) so anyone can join in one click. You can also just walk up to a teammate: when your avatars get close, a meeting link is created for both of you automatically.

    💬 Chat & Slack

    Chat in #general / #random / #team. Every message you send is mirrored to the matching Slack channel automatically, and Slack replies appear here — no extra steps. Use the search box in the chat header to filter the current channel.

    🔍 Search everything

    Press Ctrl / ⌘ K (or the 🔍 in the top-left) to search people, messages across every channel, and channels from one place. Click a result to open that person’s card or jump to that message.

    👤 Profiles

    Click any teammate in the list (or a search result) to see their card: role, team, status, break note, and Slack / Meet links.

    📝 AI Notetaker

    Open 📝 in the sidebar. Upload a recording (any language → English) or paste a transcript, set the attendees, and generate Minutes of Meeting (summary, decisions, per-person action items). Optionally post the summary to Slack and DM each owner their tasks.

    📋 Admin & 🖨 Print

    The 📋 panel (password-protected) shows live users, the attendance log with CSV export, and a one-click integrations health check (Slack / Zoho / Claude / transcription). Use 🖨 Print team overview above for a printable snapshot of who’s here and their status.
